Piano Portal

Welcome to the Piano Portal, this 14 complex note piano is controlled completely by your computer's keyboard. See the legend below for which keys control the notes, starting from the "A" as the lowest note and moving up in a zig-zag pattern to the highest note on the "L key.

Disclaimer: As a non-musical developer, the piano keys below are only an approximation for the equivalent note, just to give you a guide to what you've hit.

If you want to add any accompanying music or beats to your piano, pick out one of the backtrack buttons at the bottom of the page. They play in a continuous loop at 50% volume to your piano!
